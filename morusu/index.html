Morusu
Feel, read.
A messenger that arrives as a feeling, before words.

Morusu is a sensory messenger by plan8 Inc. You send feelings as tap rhythms — carried by vibration and bursts of color. The other person feels your message first, and reads its meaning only afterward. Meanings can be shown in English, Japanese, Spanish, Chinese, and Korean.

01
Feel
Continuous haptics on the Taptic Engine bring the rhythm to your fingertips.
02
Light
The rhythm becomes a full-screen gradient of color, so you can see it too.
03
Read
The meaning stays hidden by default. It pops up above the pattern only while you long-press it.
