MORUSU — COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Community Guidelines

Morusu is a place to send feelings — felt before words. Please help keep it safe for everyone by following these rules.

Last updated: 2026-05-27

Please do

  • Respect others. No unwanted repeated sending, stalking, or impersonation. Respect each person's receiving setting (everyone / followers only).
  • No abuse or discrimination. Do not register messages or meanings that are threatening, harassing, or discriminatory.
  • No illegal or harmful content. Sexual, violent, or illegal content, spam, and commercial promotion in the dictionary are prohibited.
  • Don't pollute the dictionary. The meaning dictionary is shared worldwide and visible to all users. Don't register meaningless entries that disrupt search, or entries far removed from their true meaning.
  • Respect rights. Quote others' names or works minimally and accurately.

If you see a violation

You can block a user from the "..." menu inside a chat, and report from the same menu. You can also email us. As a rule we respond within 24 hours, and act immediately when we judge the matter urgent.

  • Report to: hideaki@tsuruoka.me

Our response

Violating content may be removed or hidden without notice. Repeat offenders may face feature restrictions or account suspension. Setting your receiving option to "followers only" also blocks messages from strangers at the device level.
